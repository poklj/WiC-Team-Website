Introduction to Lawrencetown

COGS is located in Lawrencetown, a small village between Bridgetown and Middleton in the Annapolis Valley.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I go shopping / get groceries?

Lawrencetown itself only has a convienence store. The nearby town of Middleton has multiple grocery and convienence stores

Where is the nearest bank?

Middleton

What is there for public transportation?

King's Transit operates buses that travel through Lawrencetown. There is a bus stop located near COGS.
