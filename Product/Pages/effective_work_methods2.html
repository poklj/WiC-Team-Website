Effective Work Methods

Use To-Do Lists

Simple but effective, creating and following to do lists helps you track what needs to be done, and helps you focus on one task at a time.

Break work into smaller sections to complete

This helps you avoid overwhelming yourself, and also helps you track your progress on larger assignments.

Prioritize

Figure out what's most important and what needs to be done first, then focus on that.

Don't give yourself too much to do at a time

You can only accomplish so much in a day, and giving yourself a few tasks to complete in a day increases your chances of following through with them. If you feel up to it you can always do more afterwards, but making consistent daily progress is better than not making progress and then rushing to finish work

Create a Schedule and Routine

Plan out times to work, relax, and sleep. Having a distinct time for everything and following the schedule ensures you don't neglect your work or yourself.

Set alarms for yourself

It's easy to lose track of time, especially if you're prone to procrastinating. Setting reminders and/or alarms for yourself can get you back on track. This can also help if you tend to get caught up in your work, as a reminder to take a break from time to time.

Eliminate Distractions

Avoid any sites you aren't using for your work, leave your phone alone if possible, and close any games until you've finished.

Don't forget to take care of yourself.

You require proper sleep, hydration, and nutrition. Neglecting yourself will make impair your focus, problem-solving, motivation, and memory. Making a habit of all-nighters or regularly putting off getting something to eat until your work is finished will eventually come back to bite you.
