Music

Exercise is a well-known method of relieving stress.

Walking

Walking requires no equipment, training, or cost, and can be done at almost any time. If the weather's good, why not take a break and enjoy the outdoors a bit? Alternatively you can stretch your legs a bit around your house for a quick break when a longer walk isn't practical.

Sleep

Sometimes just having a nap can reduce stress and improve your focus and motivation. Rest allows you to come back to a problem with fresh eyes and renewed energy.
